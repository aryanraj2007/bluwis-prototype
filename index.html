SAP Solution Architect

Location: Dallas

Job Requisition #: DAL2507121

Job Title: SAP Solution Architect (10+ Years Experience)

Location: Dallas

Employment Type: Full Time

Experience Level: Senior / Principal Level

Job Summary

We are seeking a highly experienced SAP Solution Architect with over 10 years of deep expertise in SAP technologies and enterprise architecture. The ideal candidate will be a strategic thinker with hands-on experience in designing and delivering end-to-end SAP solutions that drive business value and transformation. You will work closely with cross-functional teams and stakeholders to define the future architecture of SAP landscapes across ECC, S/4HANA, and SAP BTP.

Key Responsibilities
  • Lead the design and architecture of comprehensive SAP solutions across multiple functional domains (Finance, Supply Chain, Manufacturing, Sales, Procurement).
  • Define and maintain SAP roadmap aligned with enterprise strategy, business goals, and technology standards.
  • Provide technical and functional guidance on SAP S/4HANA migrations, greenfield/brownfield implementations, and cloud adoption.
  • Collaborate with business and IT stakeholders to gather requirements and translate them into scalable, integrated SAP solutions.
  • Ensure integration with non-SAP systems through APIs, middleware (SAP PI/PO, SAP CPI), and other platforms.
  • Drive governance and best practices in SAP development, configuration, and deployment.
  • Evaluate and recommend SAP tools, add-ons, and partner solutions (SAP BTP, Fiori, RISE with SAP).
  • Mentor functional consultants and developers; act as a technical leader within the SAP delivery team.
  • Stay current with SAP innovations and roadmap, advising clients and internal teams accordingly.
Required Qualifications
  • 10+ years of hands-on experience in SAP solution design and architecture roles.
  • Expertise in SAP S/4HANA (at least one end-to-end implementation or migration project).
  • Strong knowledge of SAP modules such as FI/CO, MM, SD, PP, and/or EWM.
  • Experience in SAP integration technologies (IDoc, RFC, OData, APIs).
  • Familiarity with SAP BTP, Fiori, and SAP Analytics.
  • Deep understanding of enterprise architecture principles and TOGAF (preferred).
  • Strong communication, stakeholder management, and leadership skills.
  • Bachelor's or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business, or a related field.
  • SAP certifications (e.g., S/4HANA, Solution Architect, TOGAF) are a plus.
Preferred Skills
  • Experience with RISE with SAP and cloud deployments (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Exposure to Agile/Scrum project environments.
  • Knowledge of AI/ML use cases in the SAP ecosystem.
  • Familiarity with data migration tools (e.g., SAP Data Services, LSMW, SAP DMC).
What We Offer
  • Opportunity to work on cutting-edge SAP projects with global clients.
  • Collaborative, innovation-driven work environment.
  • Continuous learning and professional development opportunities.
  • Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits.
